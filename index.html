FAQ

Real Questions, Real Answers

We answer like a partner, not a robot. Here are the questions clients ask before they ever hop on a call.

"We've tried digital marketing before and it didn't work. Why is CHRYSM different?"

+

Most agencies focus on "vanity metrics" (likes and follows). We focus on revenue-driven strategy. We don't just post content; we build a strategic foundation that aligns your visual identity with your actual business goals. If it doesn't move the needle on your bottom line, we don't do it.

"AI and IoT sound expensive and complicated. Is it for me?"

+

Innovation shouldn't be a headache. Whether you're an early-stage founder or an established enterprise, we tailor the tech to your scale. We specialize in making complex systems feel intuitive and invisible to your end-user. Think of us as your technical translators.

"How long does a full brand redesign or website build take?"

+

Quality takes time, but we move with "startup speed." A standard high-end landing page or brand identity refresh typically takes 4–6 weeks from discovery to launch. For complex SaaS dashboards or IoT integrations, we'll provide a phased roadmap so you can start seeing results while we build the full engine.

"Will I own all the assets once the project is done?"

+

100%. Whether it's custom code, 3D illustrations, or your brand book, everything we create for you belongs to you. We even provide a "Brand Usage Guideline" and training for your team so you can maintain the high standard we set.

"What if our business model changes in a year?"

+

That's why we build for scalability. We don't use "locked" proprietary systems that force you to stay with us forever. We use flexible stacks (like Webflow or custom modular code) that allow your website and brand to grow as you do.
